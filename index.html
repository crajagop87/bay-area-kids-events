About this dashboard

About

Built by a Palo Alto parent who wanted a faster way to find things to do with the kids. A volunteer project, made for busy families.

Questions or ideas? Hit the Feedback button.

How filters work

  • Today — events happening on today's date only.
  • This weekend — Saturday and Sunday of the current week.
  • Next weekend — the following Saturday and Sunday.
  • Custom — pick any date range you like.
  • Palo Alto — events in Palo Alto, Menlo Park, Mountain View, Los Altos, and nearby Peninsula cities.
  • SF / East Bay — San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, and surrounding East Bay cities.
  • Santa Cruz — Santa Cruz, Capitola, Scotts Valley, Aptos.
  • All areas — everything across all regions.
  • My zip — enter any Bay Area zip to filter events within ~7 miles of that location.
  • Free only — filters to events with no admission cost. Some free events still require registration.

Last scraped

Events are automatically pulled every day at 6am PT from public library systems, Eventbrite, venue websites, and Bay Area family blogs. The timestamp in the header shows when the last pull completed.
